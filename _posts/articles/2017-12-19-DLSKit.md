DLS is a tiny percussion samples library of a sounds of a BDSM devices, including:

- Flogger - C2-G#2

- Belt - A2-B2

- Paddle  - C3-D#3

Format: Kontakt (v5.5 or newer required), SFZ, Ableton Live Pack.

Kontakt patch includes 8 presets and a pair of scripts.
